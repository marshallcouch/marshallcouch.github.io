Sunset Arena

A synthwave party game that transforms your Steam Deck into a coffee table arcade cabinet. Take on friends in a top-down shooter where the only control you need is a joystick. Customize, Powerup, and Derezz your way to victory. Sunset Arena is a casual party game meant to bring the party with you. With only a Steam deck you can challenge a friend. Inspired from coffee table arcade cabinets and the joys of an arcade. In Coffee Table Mode play up to 8 players with only 4 controllers. Disable coffee table mode to play up to 4 players with 4 controllers in couch mode! Simple gameplay for anyone to enjoy. Multiple control options allow for anyone to jump right into the action from single stick controls with auto-aiming, to standard twin-stick shooting.

About

Unprecedented Studios was founded by Marshall Couch. I've always felt that there aren't enough party games in the world. My favorite gaming experiences have been at lan-parties, on couches playing with friends, and games on the playground. I love the experience of gaming with others especially when it's in a shared space.
